Resources & Next Steps

Your journey doesn't end here. The key to success is continuous learning and application. Below are some resources to help you build the necessary skills and find platforms to offer your services. Start small, build a portfolio, and scale your efforts.

  • Prompt Engineering: Search for "Prompt Engineering Guides" and "Advanced Prompting Techniques" to master your ability to get the best results from Gemini.
